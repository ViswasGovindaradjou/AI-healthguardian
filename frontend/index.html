About AI Health Guardian

AI Health Guardian is an intelligent healthcare platform that helps users understand their medical reports through Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

The platform analyzes medical information and predicts risks associated with major diseases including Diabetes, Heart Disease, Kidney Disease, Liver Disease, and Stroke.

Users receive a detailed health assessment, risk visualization, personalized recommendations, and preventive health guidance in a simple and easy-to-understand format.
